Guide - Registration

Registration

Before you can access TokoKu App, you must first register. The activation code will be sent to your email upon successful registration.

  • 1. Click on the bottom section to register.
  • 2. Complete the registration form.
  • 3. Select calendar & currency to match your region
  • 4. Upon successful registration, email will be sent to registered email address.
  • 5. Provide activation code that has been sent to your email.
  • 6. You can login to TokoKu after succesful activation.

Guide - User Access

User Access

TokoKu has 2 type accounts, merchant account and user account. You will have merchant account upon registration. With merchant account you can login to TokoKu and create user account. Once you create user account, you will be access full system functionality based on it's privileged.

Field Type has values of Cashier, Waitress, Employee, Admin. By default, Cashier has access to cashier module. Waitress has access to order module. Employee has access to their commission report. Admin has access to cancel transaction.

To manage your merchant data such as product, employee and supplier, you will need to have Master Data access right under your admin user account.

  • 1. Login to TokoKu with your merchant account.
  • 2. Tap [➕] on the bottom section to add new user account.
  • 3. Complete the form and tap [✔]

Guide - Merchant Profile

Merchant Profile

Configure your merchant profile to suit your business.

Field Service Start and Service End indicate your active period to access Cloud Service for back up and sync up to another device.

Field Price Type Count allow you to define multiple pricing scheme for a product upon transaction. You can have separate price when you sell as retail, wholesale or online transaction. You can define the pricing scheme name in field Price Type 1, Price Type 2 and Price Type 3. When you create a product you can specify the value for it.

For merchant with type of Goods & Services and Foods & Beverages. Field Order Type will be available to specify when is the print out of order will be required. Whether it's before or after payment. On before payment, customer place their order first and pay later. On after payment, customer must pay first before receiving their order.

Payment by Credit can be specified in Payment Type to allow customer to receive the goods / service first and pay on another day. Customer with outstanding credit can be tracked from Credit Report module.

  • 1. Access your merchant profile.
  • 2. Select your merchant.
  • 3. Tap pencil icon on top to edit your merchant profile.

Guide - Product

Product

From menu under Master Data you can create product group and product. First you need to create product group to categorize your product for easy selection upon transaction.

Field Type has values of Goods and Service. The stock info of goods product is available under Inventory Report.

Field Person In Charge PIC required defines whether an employee need to be specified upon transaction. If it's required, you must register an employee in the Employee Module thus it can be selected upon transaction.

Field Commission defines whether a commission is given to PIC in charge of the product or service. The commision info is available in the Commission Report.

Field Promo Price, Promo Start and Promo End can be used to define promotional price for specific period.

Field Quantity Type has values of Piece (Pc), Meter and Liter. The product with meter and liter can have decimal value upon transaction.

Field Min. Stock defines minimal stock to be highlighted in the Inventory Report if the product stock fall below given limit.

  • 1. Tap [➕] on Product Group to create a new product group.
  • 2. Tap [➕] on Product Group to create a new product group.
  • 3. Complete the form and tap [✔]

Guide - Bills, Inventory & Cashflow

Bills, Inventory & Cashflow

When you purchased goods from your supplier and make the payment. The info will be stored in Bills, Inventory & Cashflow modules.

The order of storing the info is 1. Bills, 2. Inventory, 3. Cashflow.

Create bills record in the Bills module based on your invoice from the supplier. Create supplier first if it's a new supplier in the Supplier module.

Create inventory record in the Inventory module based on item purchased listed in the invoice.

Create payment record in the Cashflow module.

  • 1. Tap [➕] on Bills to create a new bill.
  • 2. Complete the form and tap [✔]
  • 3. Tap [➕] on Inventory to create a new inventory record.
  • 4. Complete the form and tap [✔]
  • 5. Tap [➕] on Cashflow to create a new cashflow record.
  • 6. Complete the form and tap [✔]

Guide - Printer

Printer

From main menu, tap Printer to configure and linked your Bluetooth Printer to TokoKu.

  • 1. Set printer status to active. Set text / line based on your printer specification.
  • 2. Bluetooth activation will be requested if the bluetooth is not yet active.
  • 3. Select from the listing. Pair it first. Standard pairing code is 0000 or 1234.
